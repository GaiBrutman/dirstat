Filter behaviour

  • Name search: non-matching tiles are dimmed to ~30% opacity (not hidden) so you keep spatial context of the full tree. Matching tiles stay full brightness.
  • Category toggle: hidden categories disappear from the treemap entirely and remaining tiles reflow to fill the space (d3 re-layout). The swatch gets struck through.
  • Both active simultaneously — they stack: category-filtered items are removed first, then name search dims within the remainder.
  • Clear search: ✕ button in the search field. Reset all categories: double-click any legend item (or a small "Reset" link appears when any are hidden).
  • Filters are not persisted across rescans — reset when a new scan starts.
